Our workflow makes all the difference.

In the field of data analytics, each project is different from the last. That's why our workflow is flexible and based on the following three pillars.

1.Strategy First

We understand that analytics are only useful when they answer the questions that arise on the workfloor or during management meetings. Fluxgate's unique combination of strategists, data scientists and designers will always come up with a solution that you and your colleagues want to use every day.

2.Quick IT Solutions

We don't like consultancy firms who bill lots of hours while coding solutions that already exist. You pay us for added value and we like to do everything as efficient as possible. We always fit our solutions to the infrastructure in place at our clients and sometimes expand with the most cost-effective tooling.
